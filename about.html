About Us

God has given us a vision of being a major epicenter of revival in the United States. We decided to go after this vision with His instruction and see all that He will accomplish in and through us. We are seeing lives healed and transformed on a regular basis here in our body of believers. Our prayer is to bring breakthrough to our world by just yielding to the Holy Spirit’s leading, presence and power.

If you are a passionate lover of Jesus and have a heart to see yourself and others, including the nations of this world, transformed for the glory of God . . . if you are not satisfied with the status quo, but are truly hungry for the supernatural interventions of God for this current time in history, then we are probably the church that you have been looking for. Come join us on the ground level to be the living stones from which the Lord can pour out a great revival and awakening!
